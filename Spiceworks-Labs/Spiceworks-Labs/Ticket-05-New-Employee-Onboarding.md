
# Ticket 05 – New Employee Onboarding

## Scenario

Human Resources submitted a request to prepare a laptop and create user accounts for a new employee starting on Monday.

---

## Issue

A new employee required a company laptop, Microsoft 365 account, email account, VPN access, and standard user permissions before their first day.

---

## Priority

Medium

---

## Status

Resolved

---

## Tasks Completed

- Verified onboarding request from Human Resources.
- Created user account.
- Assigned Microsoft 365 license.
- Configured company email account.
- Prepared Windows 11 laptop.
- Verified VPN configuration.
- Added user to the appropriate security groups.
- Confirmed workstation was ready for deployment.

---

## Resolution

All onboarding tasks were completed successfully. The employee's account was provisioned, workstation prepared, and Human Resources was notified that the equipment was ready for pickup before the employee's start date.
