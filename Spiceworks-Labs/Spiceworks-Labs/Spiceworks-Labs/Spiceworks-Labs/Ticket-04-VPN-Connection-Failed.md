# Ticket 04 – VPN Connection Failed

## Scenario

A remote employee contacted the Help Desk because they were unable to connect to the company VPN and could not access internal company resources.

---

## Issue

The user received an authentication error when attempting to establish a VPN connection. Internet connectivity was available, but access to internal systems was unavailable.

---

## Priority

High

---

## Status

Resolved

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

- Verified the user's identity.
- Confirmed the workstation had an active internet connection.
- Reviewed VPN client configuration.
- Verified user credentials.
- Restarted the VPN application.
- Refreshed user authentication.
- Tested VPN connectivity.
- Confirmed successful access to internal resources.

---

## Resolution

VPN authentication was refreshed successfully. The user connected to the VPN and verified access to internal company resources.
